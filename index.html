Process

How it works?

Digital restaurant menu enables guests in restaurants to view the menu of the restaurant and order food and drinks from their device.

Tap

Tap on nfc sticker on the table.Each table can have a single nfc tag / sticker.

Browse Menu

Customer can view the menu on their device. The menu can be customized.

Place Order

Give customer the power and time to order their meal.

Done

Focus more on preparing the food and leave the customer take thier time choosing the menu.

Features

Great features you'll love.

Users just install the app in their smartphone, enable NFC Reader and just arrive safely in the restaurant to scan the NFC reader. After that, they can discover menu available in the restaurant.

Faq

Questions and Answers.

Something is bothering you about Digital Restaurant Menu?
Here some frequently asked questions.

Where can my guests get the mobile application?

The mobile application is downloadable by the user from Apple app-store or Google play-store. From there, user can scan the NFC placed in your restaurant.

How can I update my menu?

You can easily edit, delete or add your restaurant's menu by using our Menu Management system which will be included with the Digital Restaurant Menu system.

What features are included?

The whole system will can be accessed by you, including NFC tag or sticker to be placed on your restaurant and the menu management system. The menu management system will be installed in your local PC.

Are the NFC reliable?

Near Field Communication (NFC) devices are very reliable for this system. NFC tag and sticker that are included with our package are heavy duty and long lasting. These tags are water resistant also.
